    DIFFERENCES BETWEEN PADDINGS, MARGINS AND BORDERS.


    Padding Margin Border
    Padding refers to the inner space between an element border or an element content. Margin represents the entire space outside or surrounding an element or an element border. It refers to the perimeter of the box. Depending on whatever way we decide to style our border, Borders can either be invisible, thick or thin.
